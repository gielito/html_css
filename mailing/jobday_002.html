CORPORATE NEWS
EU-Mediterranean water and agro-food partnership gets Parliament's greenlight


A new Partnership on Research and Innovation in the Mediterranean Area (PRIMA) has recently been approved by the European Parliament in Strasbourg.  PRIMA, a ten-year initiative (2018-2028) partly-funded by Horizon 2020, aims to employ sustainable solutions in areas of the Mediterranean struck by food and water shortages. The partnership currently consists of 19 EU and non-EU countries.

 
Sweden tops EU Innovation Scoreboard


Sweden once again is the EU Innovation Leader, followed by Denmark, Finland, the Netherlands, the United Kingdom, and Germany. Innovation performance has also improved in 15 EU countries; however, the Union still lags behind global innovation leaders. Carlos Moedas, Commissioner for Research, Science and Innovation, admits that still more can be done to boost research and innovation performance.  "That's why we're gearing up support to breakthrough innovators through the pilot European Innovation Council," adds Moedas.

 
129 SMEs win €6.4mn-EU funding


Of 2,165 proposals, 128 projects have been selected in the latest round of the Horizon 2020 Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) Instrument. Each will receive €50,000 to finance a feasibility study for disruptive projects, most of which are in the area of ICT, transport, and low-carbon and efficient energy systems. SMEs from Italy, Spain, and France were the most successful in this round.
EIC: Hermann Hauser to head High Level Group of Innovators


Dr Hermann Hauser KBE, co-founder of Amadeus Capital Partners and more than 20 tech companies, will chair the High Level Group of Innovators for a European Innovation Council (EIC).  His appointment was endorsed by the Group at its second meeting, which took place on 20 June 2017 at the DTU SkyLab in Denmark.  The Group was created to support the Commission in making Europe a global innovation leader.
