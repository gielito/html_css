The REAder is the REA's quarterly internal newsletter. Launched on 15th June 2009 on the occasion of the Agency's autonomy, the publication informs the staff on strategic issues and presents their activities outside the working environment.

From in-depth visionary articles to presentation of REA units, teams or activities and portraits of 'outstanding' colleagues, topics are quite wide. All colleagues can suggest articles, share their comments, or even become, for one issue or more, a REA correspondent! Do not hesitate therefore to contact the editorial board.

More punctual issues are published on the intranet and/or advertised in the e-bulletin bi-weekly electronic newsletter linked to it.
