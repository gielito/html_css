​The Financial verification team supports the work of the Agency by:

  • Ex-ante verification of the conformity of financial operations with respect to legality, regularity, internal procedures and sound financial management;
  • Follow-up of the sound financial management of administrative expenditures, contracts and grants;
  • Monitoring and reporting on commitments, expenditures and recoveries, in particular for annual activity reports and requests of the Court of Auditors and/or the European Parliament;
  • Advice, support and training (including drafting manuals);
  • Contributing to setting up strategic objectives of the unit and the specific objectives of the sector;
  • Defining financial procedures in accordance with applicable regulations and internal control standards;
  • Proposing and preparing management indicators for the monitor of the financial transactions.
  • Conducting ex-post checks on the payments to the experts and light administrative workflow;
  • Performing operational cut-off.
