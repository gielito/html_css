To become an associated event in 2016:

The call to organise the European Researchers’ Night (NIGHT) in 2016 and 2017 closed on 13 January 2016.

If you submitted a proposal that passed the evaluation thresholds but could not be funded due to insufficient call budget, you have been informed by letter of the possibility to request to associate your event with the NIGHT.

Once associated to the NIGHT, your project and its website will be advertised in the events section on the European Researchers' Night website.

The project will indicate on its website and in any promotional material (on-line and/or off-line) produced that its associated to the NIGHT event. The following wording should be used:
"The [name] project is an associated event to the European Researchers' Night initiative of the European Union funded under the Marie Skłodowska-Curie actions.
Marie Skłodowska-Curie actions
European Researchers' Night 2016-2017"
